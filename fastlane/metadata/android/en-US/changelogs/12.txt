Offline HVAC diagnosis from analog gauges + a multimeter: refrigerant charge (with target superheat computed from conditions), air-side capacity & psychrometrics, electrical checks and 13 guided troubleshooting sequences, a 0–100 health score, a text/PDF report, and an optional NOAA outdoor-temp autofill. This release enables reproducible builds — the app is developer-signed and F-Droid verifies its build matches the source, so the F-Droid and direct-download builds share one signature.
